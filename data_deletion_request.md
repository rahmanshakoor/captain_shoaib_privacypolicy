# Data Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1.  Compose an email to **support@captainshoaib.com**.
2.  Ensure you send the email from the **email address used to sign in** to the application.
3.  Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request**.

## What data will be deleted?

**Everything.**

We will permanently delete all data associated with your account, including but not limited to:
*   Personal profile information
*   App activity and history
*   User preferences
*   Any other user-associated data

## What data will be kept?

**Nothing.**

We do not retain any of your data after the deletion request is processed.
