# Privacy Policy for Captain Shoaib App

**Last updated:** January 06, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Captain Shoaib ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. By using our App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our App, including:

### A. Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect the following personal details:
*   **Name**: To identify you within the system.
*   **Email Address**: For account authentication, communication, and password recovery.
*   **Physical Characteristics**: Weight, Height, Age, and Gender. This data is essential to generate personalized workout and nutrition plans.
*   **Contact Information**: Phone number and address (if provided).

### B. Health and Fitness Data
To provide our core fitness coaching services, we collect:
*   **Medical History**: Information about injuries or medical conditions to ensure workout safety.
*   **Lifestyle Data**: Hours of sleep, hours of work, and daily activity levels.
*   **Fitness Goals**: Your enrollment year and specific targets.
*   **Workout Performance**: Logs of your exercises, sets, reps, and streak data.

### C. Media and Photos
*   **Body Assessment Photos**: You may upload photos of your physique to allow our coaches to assess your progress and adjust your plans.
*   **Transaction Photos**: We may request photos of payment receipts or transactions to verify subscriptions.

### D. Device and Usage Information
We may automatically collect certain information when you access the App, including:
*   Device type and operating system.
*   Internet Protocol (IP) address.
*   App usage patterns and crash reports.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
*   **Service Provision**: To create and deliver personalized workout routines, nutrition plans, and supplement recommendations.
*   **Progress Tracking**: To monitor your physical changes and streaks over time.
*   **Account Management**: To manage your registration, login, and subscription status.
*   **Communication**: To send you updates, potential adjustments to your plans, or administrative information.
*   **Improvement**: To analyze usage trends and improve the functionality and user experience of our App.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:
*   **Service Providers**: With third-party service providers (e.g., hosting services, analytics) who assist us in operating the App and conducting our business.
*   **Legal Requirements**: If required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or prevent fraud.

## 4. Permissions

To function correctly, our App requires the following permissions:
*   **Internet Access**: To communicate with our servers (`captainshoaib.com`) to sync your profile, plans, and progress.
*   **Camera and Photo Gallery**: To allow you to take or select photos for body assessments and subscription verification.
*   **Storage**: To temporarily store your profile data and plans for offline access (using local storage).

## 5. Data Retention and Security

*   **Security**: We implement reasonable security measures, including HTTPS encryption for data transmission, to protect your information.
*   **Retention**: We retain your personal information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
*   Access the personal information we hold about you.
*   Request the correction of inaccurate information.
*   Request the deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the support email provided in the App.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
